Registration Procedures

You can register to our conference by filling in the Copyright form (for full CCIS paper only), and send us the filled form through e-mail (icte@hksmic.org.hk). Please pay your registration fee based on the registration details in an earlier email conversation (a separate email of the paper acceptance notification).

You can register to our conference by filling in the Registration form and the Copyright form (for full paper only), and send us the filled form(s) through e-mail to Dr Kwok Tai Chui (ICTE 2025 Organizing Chair) (jktchui@hkmu.edu.hk). Please pay your registration fee (in the currency Hong Kong Dollars) via Telegraphic Transfer and send us the proof of transaction together with the filled form(s).
