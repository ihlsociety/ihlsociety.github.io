Transportation Routes

1.1 (Subway) From the airport: Take Line 11, transfer to Line 14, then transfer to Line 16 to Huilongpu Station. From Exit A, take a taxi to the school, which is 2.7 kilometres away. The whole journey takes about 2 hours.

1.2 (Subway) Shenzhen North Railway Station: Take Line 5, transfer to Line 14, then transfer to Line 16 to Huilongpu Station.

2.1 (Taxi) The journey from the airport to Shenzhen City Polytechnic is approximately 65km and takes about 1.5-2 hours.

2.2 (Taxi) The journey from Shenzhen North Railway Station to Shenzhen City Polytechnic is approximately 36 kilometers and takes about 1 hour.
